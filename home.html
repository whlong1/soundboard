Welcome

Soundboard is an online repository of music information.

Soundboard utilizes the TheAudioDB API to organize information on recording artists in a user-friendly interface.

To find an artist's biography, full discography, and even information on individual albums, please visit the artists section.

To find an up to date list of the top trending albums on iTunes, please visit the charts section.
